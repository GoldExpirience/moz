Other Tours
Watch Out for Tourist Traps!

 

  • Sub-standard hotels or hotels on the outskirts of the city (or both)
  • Poorly planned itineraries with half of your time wasted on the bus or in lines
  • Objectively poor program to keep you away from best (read expensive) sights. Imagine going to Paris and skipping the Eiffel Tower.
  • Unlicensed guides or "tour directors" (which is actually illegal)
  • Below standard customization options - who cares that you are going to visit more than one country
  • Visas not included - you go figure it out on your own
  • Unprofessional customer service and unreliable in-trip support
  • Initially unmentioned fees and additional charges
  • Shady companies hiding their legal names, locations, and insurance coverage
